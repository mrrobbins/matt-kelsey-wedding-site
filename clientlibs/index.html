Our Love Story

How We Met

It was their senior year of High School in AP Physics. Matt had his eye on Kelsey, and told her one day that she looked like 'that girl from Grey's Anatomy' (Katherine Hiegl). She took it as a complement and thought nothing else of it. Two weeks before graduation, Mr. Kledzik, their AP Physics teacher told Kelsey that he thought she and Matt would be a good fit - this sparked Kelsey's interest and she sought Matt out to chat. The rest, as they say, is history.

Our First Date

Matt and Kelsey has their first date on Memorial Day weekend, 2008. They went for a hike in Grant Nature Park in Centerville, and talked for hours about anything and everything. They sparked an interest and didn't want to stop talking, so they continued to Chipotle for lunch. Kelsey barely ate any of her burrito because she didn't want Matt to think she was a slob, and Matt thought Kelsey might be a little strange for not liking Chipotle!

Getting Engaged

Matt gave Kelsey a hot air balloon ride for her birthday. When it came time to get in the balloon, Matt and Kelsey were told they might have to ride in two separate balloons because of the weather - Matt alerted to the coordinator that this simply wouldn't do! It worked out, and Matt and Kelsey got assigned to the Soccer Balloon. While up in the balloon, Matt asked Kelsey to marry him, and she said Yes! Matt then exuberantly shouted to the houses below that they had just gotten engaged. It was truly magical
